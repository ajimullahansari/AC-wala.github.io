Frequently Asked Questions

1️⃣How often should I service my RO purifier?

It is recommended to service your RO water purifier every 3 to 6 months for optimal performance.

2️⃣What are the common signs that my RO needs repair?

Some common signs include reduced water flow, unusual taste or odor, leakage, and unusual noises from the system.

3️⃣How long does an RO filter last?

On average, RO filters should be replaced every 6 to 12 months, depending on water quality and usage.
