Frequently Asked Questions

1️⃣ Cooler ki servicing kitne din me ek baar karwani chahiye?

✔️ Har 2-3 mahine me ek baar servicing karwana best hota hai, specially garmi ke season me.

2️⃣ Cooler me se smell aa rahi hai, iska kya solution hai?

✔️ Cooling pads aur water tank me kachra ya algae jamne se badbu aati hai. Unko saaf karein ya replace karein.

3️⃣ Cooler pani kyu leak kar raha hai?

✔️ Iske reasons ho sakte hain: Cooling pads jyada gile ho gaye hain Water tank me crack ya leakage hai Drainage system block ho gaya hai Pump me koi issue ho sakta hai

4️⃣ Cooler thandi hawa nahi de raha, kya karein?

✔️ Cooling pads check karein, agar ghis gaye hain toh replace karein. ✔️ Water level sahi rakhein aur pump check karein. ✔️ Fan motor me problem ho sakti hai.

5️⃣ Cooler se awaaz bahut aa rahi hai, iska kya solution hai?

✔️ Motor ya fan blades loose ho sakti hain. Unko tight karein ya replace karein. ✔️ Bearings ya belt ka maintenance karwana pad sakta hai.
